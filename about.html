ABOUT US

Embrace Botswana's wilderness through sustainable adventures.

Our Story at Khwai Nomadic Campgrounds, we are passionate about sharing the wonders of Botswana's wilderness with our guests. Our campgrounds are thoughtfully designed to provide a harmonious experience where nature meets comfort. From guided safaris to cultural immersions, every aspect of our camp is crafted to offer an authentic and enriching journey. We are proud to promote sustainable tourism, ensuring that our operations benefit the environment and the local communities. Join us to discover the magic of Khwai.
