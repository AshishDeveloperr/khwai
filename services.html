Our Services

Experience Botswana: Camping, Game Drives, Walks, Dance, Cruises

01

Camping Grounds

Khwai Nomadic Campgrounds offers an extraordinary retreat just 5 kilometers from the charming Khwai Village in Botswana. Nestled along the serene Mbundi River, a tributary of the iconic Khwai River, this destination immerses visitors in the breathtaking beauty of the African wilderness. The campgrounds are thoughtfully designed to provide a harmonious blend of comfort and nature's rugged charm.

02

Boat Cruise

Set sail on a picturesque boat cruise along the Khwai and Mbundi Rivers. This leisurely adventure provides stunning views of the waterways, abundant birdlife, and wildlife along the riverbanks. Our boat cruises are perfect for photographers and nature lovers alike and offer a unique perspective of the region's beauty. As you drift along the calm waters, immerse yourself in the serenity and wonder of this unspoiled paradise. Every moment on the river is an opportunity to create unforgettable memories.

03

Traditional Dance

Delight the vibrant rhythms and movements of Botswana's traditional dances during your stay at Khwai Nomadic Campgrounds. Our performances showcase the rich cultural heritage of the local communities, blending storytelling with music and dance. Join in the festivities and experience the joy and energy of these cultural expressions. These dances not only entertain but also provide a meaningful connection to the traditions and history of the region.

04

Mokoro Trails

Glide through the tranquil waterways of the Okavango Delta on a traditional mokoro canoe. Our mokoro trails offer a serene and intimate way to explore the region's unique ecosystems. Guided by skilled polers, you'll have the chance to observe aquatic wildlife, water lilies, and the breathtaking scenery up close. This iconic Botswana experience is perfect for those seeking peace and connection with nature, surrounded by the gentle sounds of the water and the whispers of the reeds.
